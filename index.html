About

Hi there.


I am a Web an UX Designer based in San Francisco.


I love beautiful and intricate design, but also appreciate the simplicity and quiet elegance in subtle strokes.


I graduated with an MA in Web Design and New Media from the Academy of Art University and fell in love with building websites and web applications using great and cognizant design as base.


With my inquisitive mind, I constantly seek learning experiences through mentorship and team work and expanding my creativity through inspiration and experience.
